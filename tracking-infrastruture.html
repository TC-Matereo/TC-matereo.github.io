MATEREO SHM

Safety Through Smart tracking Technologies

Matereo SHM is a data analytic web platform with structural integrity indicators designed for infrastruture managers. It continuously integrates data from installed sensors and weather.

WHY CHOOSE MATEREO SHM PLATFORM

✔ Multi-Source Integration: Combines in-situ sensors and third-party data.

✔ Damage indicators: Alerts based on historical beavior, data analytics and structural thresholds.

✔ Costum Dashboards and API: Get real-time safety insights tailored to your organization.

WHAT PARAMETERS DOES IT MEASURE?

Displacement & Deformation – Detects shifts, subsidence, and instability.

Vibration – Monitors changes in structural response.

Temperature & Thermal Expansion – Assesses environmental stress.
